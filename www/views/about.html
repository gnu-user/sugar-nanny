What is Sugar Nanny?

Diabetes affects millions of people worldwide, Sugar Nanny is a web application intended to aid those suffering from diabetes. Just like how a spoon full of sugar helps the medicine go down, Sugar Nanny helps with regulating your blood sugar and taking insulin when needed.

Sugar Nanny was started as the result of the daily struggle our friends have, whom suffer from both Type I and Type II diabetes. Often they have challenges maintaining a healthy blood sugar level, and making a habit of checking their blood sugar regularly.

We hope that Sugar Nanny will help impact your life and make a difference, the same way that it has for our friends. Think of it as your personal nanny, keeping track of your health and reminding you to check your blood sugar regularly.
